An annual trip to Silicon Valley where Penn students meet with prominent Venture Capitalists, Founders, and Alumni.

The Experience

Every year 20 of Penn’s most entrepreneurial students take a trip to the San Francisco Bay Area to meet with the Silicon Valley’s most prominent figures. Participants gain an inside look at the culture of large tech companies, emergent startups, and prominant venture capital firms — heard driectly from their founders and executive leadership. Now in its fifth year, the Tech Trek has met with a diverse group of over 50 induviduals, including Sal Khan, Andy Radcliff, First Last, First Last, and First Last.

The Speakers

Over the course of 4 days, participants in the Penn Tech Trek will visit over 20 companies and speak with over 50 prominent members of the technology industry. This trip is not just a series of recruiting presentations at different companies. Rather, the Tech Trek includes private meetings with the founders, chief officers, venture capitalists, and engineers working at the valley’s most exciting companies. While the trip includes many of the most famous companies in tech, it also focuses on emerging startups and Penn Alumni projects.

The Students

As a school still rooted in the entrepreneurial and multidisciplinary ideals of Benjamin Franklin, the University of Pennsylvania has some of the brightest students in the country. The Penn Tech Trek includes a curated sample of these students, admitted via application from the Engineering School, College, and Wharton. Their interests include the technical, operational, managerial, and cultural aspects of starting, growing, and running a technology focused company.The Penn Tech Trek enables these students to make life-long connections, learn from leaders in tech, and eventually imagine a life for themselves in Silicon Valley.

The Application

A large number of students demonstrate interest in the Penn Tech Trek, and the organizers wish to bring together a diverse group for the trip. Application for the trip includes an application and interview for finalist applicants.


Applications for the Penn Tech Trek will open in early August. If you would like to get updates on the trip please like our Facebook Page and register for our info session.
